### What's new

- **Play Store ready** — SweetSpot is now distributed via Google Play with a 14-day free trial and a one-time purchase to unlock permanently.
- **Paywall screen** — after the trial ends, a full-screen paywall prompts you to unlock or restore a previous purchase.
- **Trial countdown** — the main screen shows how many trial days remain.
- **Watch locked state** — the Wear OS app shows a message to unlock from the phone when the trial expires.
- **AAB builds** — `make bundle` now builds Android App Bundles for both phone and watch, ready for Play Store upload.
